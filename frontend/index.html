Rice Support System with Rain Forecasting

AgriRain is a decision-support system designed to help rice farmers make smarter farming decisions using rain forecast and weather data.

About Us

AgriRain is a smart rain forecasting system designed to help farmers make better decisions.

Provides accurate weather insights, crop planning support, resource optimization, productivity improvement, and aims to connect technology with sustainable agriculture.

How It Works

Select your area to get rain info, localized to exact weather.

Choose a specific date to view the expected weather conditions.

View the forecast results, including rainfall predictions and insights for planning.
